Translation Work

We need people who can help translate material into different languages.

Most of all we need Kurdish translators. There is a lot of information that can help Rojava's society massively but it needs to be translated to Kurdish. Also people are needed who can translate material from Kurmanci, Arabic or Turkish into English, Mandarin, Hindu, French, Spanish and other widely spoken languages. Finally we need Assyrian/Aramaean translators.

Please contact us if you are able to translate between languages.

Specialist Advice and Help

Promotion, Networking and Street Campaigning

Hit the streets, spread the Rojava Plan globally, get campaigning and promote us on social media. We want everyone to see what is happening in Rojava.

If you know affinity groups, then make sure they know about us. Make the connection. We are looking for friends to link up with around the world who share our goals.
