About Rojava

Rojava is an autonomous region in north Syria. The region gained its autonomy in November 2013 in the process of the Syrian uprising, establishing a society based on principles of direct democracy, gender equity, and sustainability. Rojava consists of the three cantons called Jazira, Kobani and Afrin.

For more info on Rojava, see the Wikipedia entry or our resources page.
