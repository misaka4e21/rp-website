What's The Plan?

In 2 years we are going to implement these projects. Bringing the latest decentralized solutions to Rojava.

Organic fertilizer production

We will solve Rojava's food shortage problems. Every year Rojava imports 150 million dollars of fertilizer that can be easily produced locally.

See the Wiki page →

Revolution Media

Lets build the world biggest and best revolutionary media from inside the heart of the revolution!

See the Wiki page →

Internet and telephone system

Internet and telephone is a big problem here. We will solve this by building a Rojava community network, following the experience of Guifi.Net

See the Wiki page →

Natural medicine

Discover pure local medicines. Lets research this ecology and find new natural cures. We can stop the embargo against good health - everyone deserves to be healthy.

See the Wiki page →

Earthbag construction

Earthbag (or superadobe) is a low-cost ecological method of building nice houses with natural temperature regulation. It uses local materials. Perfect for a country under embargo where concrete is very expensive.

See the Wiki page →

Kurdish Ubuntu

We are making the world's first Kurdish operating system, and developing a school curriculum based off free software. We need hackers and free software enthusiasts.

See the Wiki page →

Solar panels

Solar panels become cheaper and better every year. We are creating a fabrication plant in Rojava to solve Rojava's electrical needs in an ecological way.

See the Wiki page →

3D printing

We need 3D printers! 3D printers can create replacement arms and legs, tools for labs and schools, and even make metal moulds. There are many possibilities, and Rojava needs this tech.

See the Wiki page →

Plastic greenhouses

Rojava has a water problem. It doesn't help that Turkey is building dams. These greenhouses trap water inside, and allows more variety of crops to be grown.

See the Wiki page →

Education projects

The education system here is starting from zero. This is an opportunity to create a new pedagogy for all subjects.

See the Wiki page →

Moringa trees

Moringa is a wonder plant with many uses. We want to introduce Moringa to this environment, and stop the growing desert.

See the Wiki page →

Biogas production

Generating gas for cooking and heating from domestic waste.

See the Wiki page →
