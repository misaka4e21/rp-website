Protect Your Emails with Encryption Software

Encryption software protects your communications. It prevents malicious groups from intercepting your messages and reading them. We recommend you learn to use encryption methods for your own safety.

For your safety, we recommend encrypted communication through PGP. Here are some guides for common systems people use:

  • Windows
  • Mac OS X
  • Android

A less secure option is the Mailvelope plugin for Chrome and Firefox.
